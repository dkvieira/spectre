Detailed Description

template<size_t Dim>
struct ScalarWave::ComputeNormalDotFluxes< Dim >

Compute normal component of flux on a boundary.

\begin{align} F(\Psi) =& 0 \\ F(\Pi) =& n^i \Phi_i \\ F(\Phi_i) =& n_i \Pi \end{align}
