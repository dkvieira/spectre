19 /// \ingroup CoordinateMapsGroup
20 /// Two dimensional map from the unit square to a wedge, which is constructed
21 /// by interpolating between a circular arc of radius `radius_of_circle` and a
22 /// flat face which is circumscribed by a circular arc of radius
23 /// `radius_of_other`. These arcs extend \f$\pi/2\f$ in angle, and can be
24 /// oriented along the +/- x or y axis. The choice of using either equiangular
25 /// or equidistant coordinates along the arcs is specifiable with
26 /// `with_equiangular_map`. For a more detailed discussion, see the
27 /// documentation for Wedge3D.
